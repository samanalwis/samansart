Wine by the Harbour

Price: $500

Materials used: Soft Pastel, Pastel Pencil

Size: 297mm x 420mm

Description: The painting captures a serene moment of a woman enjoying a peaceful escape. Dressed in a loose, white flowy dress, she sits gracefully on the edge of a balcony. Her hair is loosely tied up in a bun, with a few stray strands softly framing her face. She holds a glass of red wine in one hand, savoring the moment as she gazes out over the breathtaking scene before her. Below, the turquoise ocean stretches endlessly, sparkling under the sun, while small boats drift gently on the water.
