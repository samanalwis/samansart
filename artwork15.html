The Poor Man

Materials used: Oil Pastel

Size:297mm x 420mm

Description:A bearded beggar, his weathered face etched with deep wrinkles, stands with outstretched hands. His eyes reflect profound sadness and hardship, telling stories of struggle. The long, unkempt beard adds to his aged, weary appearance. His hands, empty and trembling, express silent pleas for help, capturing the viewer’s empathy. The artwork evokes raw emotion and vulnerability.
