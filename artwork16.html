The Tanzanian Man

Price: $250

Materials used: Oil Pastel

Size: 297mm x 420mm

Description:A Tanzanian man stands tall, smiling confidently at an angle. His long dreadlocks, neatly held back by a mustard-colored headband, cascade down his back. A bright red fabric drapes elegantly across his torso, complemented by beaded necklaces that reflect his cultural heritage. The artwork captures his warmth, pride, and strength, highlighting his serene expression and traditional attire up to his waist, evoking a sense of joy and cultural richness.
