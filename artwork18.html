The Cracked Flower Vase

Materials used: Soft Pastel, Pastel Pencil

Size: 297mm x 420mm

Description:A cracked purple flower vase, delicately worn, holds a stunning bouquet of mixed leafy flowers. Vibrant greens and soft pastels emerge from the vase, contrasting its weathered surface. The intricate cracks on the vase tell a story of time, while the flowers inside bloom with life and color, symbolizing resilience and beauty. The artwork captures the harmony between fragility and renewal, with the flowers’ lush elegance contrasting the vase’s imperfect charm.
