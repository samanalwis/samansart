The Woods

SOLD

Materials used: Soft Pastel, Pastel Pencil

Size: 297mm x 420mm

Description: The artwork focuses on the dense, textured trunks of Australian woods, with eucalyptus trees standing tall amidst rich foliage. The deep browns and grays of the bark contrast with vibrant green leaves. The scene evokes a serene, earthy atmosphere, showcasing the beauty and stillness of the Australian forest.
