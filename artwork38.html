Wine by the Harbour-Artwork Details

Ho Chi Minh

Materials used: Charcoal on Canvas

Size: 310mm x 390mm

Description: This artwork portrays Ho Chi Minh in a contemplative expression, highlighting his sharp features, iconic beard, and serene demeanor. The background features a muted, earthy palette with subtle elements of Vietnam's landscapes and revolution symbols, creating a powerful contrast that embodies his enduring legacy and the country's journey toward independence.
